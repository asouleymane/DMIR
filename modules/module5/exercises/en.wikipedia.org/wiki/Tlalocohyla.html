Tlalocohyla is a genus of frogs in the Hylidae family. This genus was created in 2005 following a major revision of the Hylidae family.[1] The four species in this genus were previously placed in the Hyla genus.

Species[edit]

Binomial name and author Common name
T. godmani (Günther, 1901) Godman's tree frog
T. loquax (Gaige and Stuart, 1934) mahogany tree frog
T. picta (Günther, 1901) painted tree frog
T. smithii (Boulenger, 1902) dwarf Mexican tree frog
