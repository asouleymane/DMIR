Xenohyla is a genus of frog in the Hylidae family, and are found in the Brazilian states of Bahia and Rio de Janeiro.

Species[edit]

Only two species are recognized in this genus:

  • Xenohyla eugenioi Caramaschi, 1998
  • Xenohyla truncata (Izecksohn, 1959) - Izecksohn's Brazilian tree frog
